Who Is Pretty Boi Drag?

Pretty Boi Drag is a BIPOC-centered drag king performance troupe based in Washington, DC. Our mission is to create safe, inclusive spaces for anyone who wants to explore masculine performance or drag kinging.

What Is A Drag King?

A drag king is most often defined as a female-bodied person who performs an interpretation of masculinty in front of an audience in a variety of ways including: lip-syncing, live singing, stand-up, emceeing and sideshow.

There is no right or wrong way to be a drag king. Masculinity can be interpreted, and therefore performed, differently from king to king. We highly encourage new performers to experiment with what masculinity means to them and how they would like the audience to be affected by their performance.

What Supplies Do I Need?

These are just a few supplies that a drag king may have in their bag:

  • Makeup Wipes - For clean up during and after a show. Available at most drug stores and makeup stores.
  • Eyebrow Brush - Used to accentuate eyebrows as well as clean up lines for beards and mustaches. Available at most drug stores and makeup stores.
  • Eyeliner - Used to either draw on mustaches and beards or create lines in the face.
  • Foundation - Apply to face with makeup sponge for either basic coverage or to apply fine hairs to the face (ie 5 oâ€™clock shadow). Available at most drug stores and makeup stores.
  • Makeup Brush(es) - Apply stage makeup or use to apply or clear away loose hairs on the face. There are lot of different types of makeup brushes, choose the best one for you. Available at most drug stores and makeup stores.
  • Makeup Sponge - Use to apply stage makeup. Available at most drug stores and makeup stores.
  • Hair (crepe or real) - Crepe hair is fake stage hair you can manipulate and use to apply to face with glue. Often performers will use their real hair which looks more realistic and sticks to the face better with glue.
  • Prosthetic beards or mustaches - These a pre-made beards and mustaches that can be applied to the face with glue. Available from a variety of online retailers, costume shops and Halloween or party supply stores.
  • Glue - Either spirit gum, eyelash glue or medical adhesive. Anything that is water-based, non-toxic, non-irritating and latex free is preferable. Available on Amazon or drug store (eyelash glue).
  • Binder - Any variety of constrictive materials used to flatten breasts. Not necessary, but often used to create the illusion of a cis male body. Available at many online retailers.
  • Packer - A packer can be a variety of items to wear as padding or a phallic object in the front of the pants or underwear to give the appearance of having a penis and male bulge. Also not necessary, but used by some kings to complete the illusion. Available online and at adult product retail stores.
  • Wig - Some kings may wear a wig to complete a specific illusion or to hide longer hair. Available at many beauty supply stores and online.

There may be many more items in a kings bag based on their persona, performance style and the depth of their illusion. Feel free to experiment with some or all of these items!
