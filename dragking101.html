What is a drag king?

Most often, a drag king is a female-bodied male impersonator who performs in front of an audience in a variety of ways including: lip-syncing, live singing, stand-up, emceeing and sideshow.

There is no right or wrong way to be a drag king. Pretty Boi Drag often focuses on hyper-masculine performers and performances, but masculinity can be interpreted, and therefore performed, differently from king to king.
